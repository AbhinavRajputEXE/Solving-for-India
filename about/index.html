About us

Welcome to KhetiFy, your go-to destination for all things related to farming and agriculture. Our team of skilled computer science engineers, known as the Web Wizards, have come together to create a platform that aims to revolutionize the way farmers interact with technology. With KhetiFy, farmers can easily manage their inventory, access information about crops and farming techniques, and connect with a community of like-minded individuals who share their passion for agriculture. Our AI chat feature is designed to provide farmers with personalized assistance and advice, making farming a more streamlined and efficient process. At KhetiFy, we believe in empowering farmers with the tools and resources they need to thrive, and we are committed to creating a brighter future for the farming community.
